BNF grammar notation
Backus-Naur Form notation. A meta-syntax for describing context-free grammars. See the BNF article on Wikipedia for more details.
continued
A continued gesture is just one that’s been continued since the prior utterance. Such gestures should be marked with a tilde (~).
disjoint prepositional phrase
A prepositional phrase in which the object of the preposition is fronted (For example: Is that what you were sitting on? Which box should we put it in?)
