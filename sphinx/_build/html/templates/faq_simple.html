1. How do I make a simple FAQ?¶

To make a simple FAQ just use this template and modify it. Replace this question and answer with your own and duplicate as necessary.

Note that _my-faq should be changed to some unique name for your FAQ so you and others can refer to it in other parts of the collected documentation. For example, we can link to the Transcription Guide FAQ because it’s uniquely identified as tg-faq.
